Hi! I am Aniket Vashishtha, a Pre-Doctoral Fellow at Microsoft Research India (MSRI), where I work with Dr. Amit Sharma on evaluating Causal Understanding and Reasoning capabilities in Large Language Models. My research centers on assessing the potential of LLMs for causal reasoning, serving as pseudo domain experts for inference in fields such as Healthcare. Prior to this, I worked with Dr. Sunayana Sitaram and Dr. Monojit Choudhary in the NLP team at MSR India, specifically focusing on how to make Language Models more inclusive and less biased. My worked concentrated on extending Responsible AI methods for bias evaluation and mitigation techniques to less popular and low resource languages with an emphasis on Indian Languages.
I am broadly interested in developing fair, performant and inclusive machine learning systems for real-world deployments.
I graduated with a B.Tech (Hons.) in Information Technology from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi, India in 2022. For more details, check my CV or hit me up on my email.
